What can an advocate do?

o Speak for you and represent your views when you feel unable to do this yourself.

o Provide information and guidance.

o Prepare written communication or legal submissions.

o Attend meetings, hearings or appointments.

o Clarify processes and procedures.

o Negotiate, mediate or otherwise assist in alternative dispute resolution.

o Gather and organize information and documentation.
